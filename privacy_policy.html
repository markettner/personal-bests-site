Your Privacy is Protected

Personal Bests does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Personal Bests does not collect any data from its users. We have designed our app with privacy as a core principle.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No third-party services have access to your data

On-Device Processing

All data processing within Personal Bests occurs entirely on your device. Your workout data from Apple Health stays on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

The app only reads workout data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission to display your personal records. This data is processed locally and is never stored outside of the standard system frameworks.

Apple HealthKit

Personal Bests integrates with Apple HealthKit to access your workout data. This integration is governed by Apple's strict privacy guidelines:

  • You control which data the app can access
  • HealthKit data is encrypted and protected by iOS
  • We only read data; we never write to HealthKit
  • Access can be revoked at any time in Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Our commitment to not collecting user data will remain unchanged.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
